FAQs

How do I get to the Cisco Cubao bus terminal?



Take the LRT-2 or MRT-3 train until the Araneta Center Cubao Station. From either stations, it will take 6 minutes or less to walk to Cisco's Cubao Terminal. This terminal is shared with Five Star Transport and the rest of Cisco's sister bus companies.



Do I get a fare discount rate as a student or senior?



Child, Student, Senior, and PWD discounts of 20% apply for tickets bought from the bus terminal only.



Can I rent a Cisco Bus for private purposes?



Cisco Bus rent is only available at the bus terminal only.



Is it possible to get a refund?



The refund is sought following the occurrence of bus journey cancellations and route suspensions for reasons other than safety and security reasons (as determined by our bus company) or when justified by circumstances beyond the Cisco bus' control or delays or diversions in the journey. Other reason that is not justified by circumstances beyond the Cisco bus is not applicable for refund.
